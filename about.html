Hello, I'm Ara

I'm a software developer with experience and interest in art and culture. Currently based in New York City, I predominantly grew up in Seoul and have also lived in Moscow and Chicago. I love creating things from scratch. Drawing and painting are my default languages to communicate with otherss, and coding is my act of creation. For me, coding is the most developed way to integrate aesthetics into the everyday lives of people and their culture. Because everybody approaches a problem differently, problem solving doesnâ€™t only reflect technique but also your personality and your dedication to serve up to the society you are in -- this fascinates me.

As a full stack software developer, I have extensive experience with React and Redux with JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, HTML5 and CSS3. I have also given lectures on these and other technologies including jQuery, Git, RSpec, and SQL as a part of my work as instructor at App Academy. Integrating my artist career, I am eagerly researching on how to integrate art and technologies. My ultimate goal as both software developer and artist is to bridge these two genres and make it more accessible to others.

My hobby is writing in Korean. As a fun fact about myself, I published 4 novels and 1 essay book as hard copies through official Korean publishers. Additional Skills are Mongoose, MongoDB, Node.js, Express.js, SQLite3, Webpack, jQuery, Git, Heroku, AWS.
